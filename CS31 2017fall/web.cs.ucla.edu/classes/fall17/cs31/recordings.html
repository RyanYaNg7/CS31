CS 31 Old Recorded Lectures

These are CS 31 lectures recorded in Fall 2013. They are most of the lectures from that quarter, although a few scattered ones are missing. Since these are recordings from an earlier offering of the course, you should, of course, ignore any particular dates mentioned (e.g., for exams).

The Bruincast people say that these are best viewed with the Safari or Edge browsers. They tell you how to enable Adobe Flash for the CCLE site so that you can view them with Chrome. They also say that if you're using a UCLA wireless network, use UCLA_WIFI or eduroam, not UCLA_WEB.

For reasons I don't know, sometimes trying to start viewing a lecture gives an "Error loading media: File could not be played" message, but clicking on that message causes it to try again and it succeeds (or if it doesn't, clicking on it yet again works).

There is a problem with our providing a direct link to the videos, so you'll have to log to CCLE, go to the CS 31 page, and select "Media resources" from the left column. The dates in the "Course date" column are approximately one week later than the actual date the material in the corresponding video was presented in class (so for example, the video labeled "Wed, 10/25/2017" is actually material presented Wednesday, October 18).
